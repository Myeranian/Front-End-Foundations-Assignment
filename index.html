About Me

I am currently enrolled in the Thinkful Engineering Bootcamp, and I am very excited to jump into the world of web development! My proficiencies include HTML, CSS, Javascript, Node, React, and UI design. I am super excited about the prospect of being on a team of skilled programmers and designers working together to create effective and beautiful products! My aim is to bring a high-energy work ethic to anything I work on. I never shy away from asking questions or from listening to answers.

Some of my other interests are gardening, hiking, drawing, and making indoor and outdoor sculptures. I also enjoy a good local beer.
